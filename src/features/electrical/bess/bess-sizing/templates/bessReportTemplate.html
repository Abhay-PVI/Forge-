Project Electrical Scheme

The project has a capacity of {{projectCapacityMW}} MW / {{projectCapacityMWh}} MWh. The DC system will operate at a maximum of {{dcVoltage}} V. The MV collection system will operate at {{mvVoltage}} kV, and the main power transformer will step up the voltage to {{poiVoltage}} kV for interconnection.

{{clientName}} has chosen {{bessManufacturer}} and {{pcsManufacturer}} PCS solution for this project.

The battery rack minimum and maximum voltages are {{batteryMinVoltage}} Vdc and {{batteryMaxVoltage}} Vdc respectively. The {{bessManufacturer}} Enclosure uses a standard {{ipRating}} rated enclosure. The liquid cooling system of the enclosure maintains the temperature between {{tempMin}} to {{tempMax}} deg C. The enclosure includes all primary DC wiring, fusing, SPD, disconnects and monitoring required to safely exchange power between battery enclosure and PCS.

The {{pcsManufacturer}} PCS System {{pcsModel}} is rated at {{pcsRating}} kVA, while the MVT is rated at {{mvtRating}} kVA. The MVT includes looping provisions up to {{maxMvtLoop}} units. The final feeder connects to the plant substation. At {{mvVoltage}} kV substation {{noOfVCB}} VCB feeders are used. A fuse and disconnector switch is used to connect auxiliary feeder to the plant substation.
