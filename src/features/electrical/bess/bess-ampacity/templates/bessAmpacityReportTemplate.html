Applicable Formulas

Voltage Drop Formula:

V% drop = (1.732 × k × L × I) / (Cm × 1000 × N × V)

Where:

  • I – Max Current from MV Transformer
  • L – Length of the conductor in feet
  • k – Conductor resistivity in Ω/KCMIL·feet (21.2 for aluminum)
  • Cm – Cross section area of conductor (Circular mils)
  • V – Operating Voltage
  • N – Number of Parallel conductors in Circuit
